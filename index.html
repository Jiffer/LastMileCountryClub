About

Last Mile Country Club plays a blend of bluegrass and Cosmic Americana with one foot in tradition and the other in outlaw territory. Formed in 2023, what started as a straight bluegrass outfit found a new orbit with the addition of drums and pedal steel. Our lineup features versatile multi-instrumentalists rotating through guitar, mandolin, banjo, bass, steel, and drums. With members drawn from Boulder County bands Sun Jr., The Hop Pickers, Colorado Junction, and Legitimate Front, the current lineup shares their music from brewery patios, packed barrooms, and festival stages. We're equally at home playing a bluegrass train song or grooving some country funk. Originals and covers span alt-country, bluegrass, slacker rock and honky-tonk, built on a shared love of Neil Young, Gram, My Morning Jacket, and late-night jams. Hula hoops and conga lines encouraged.
